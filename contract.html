AMS Property Management

Contract No. AMS-2025-0301

This Contract of Lease (hereinafter referred to as the “Agreement”) is entered into and executed this 28th day of February 2025, in the City of Quezon, Philippines, by and between:

AMS PROPERTY MANAGEMENT, a property management entity duly organized and existing under the laws of the Republic of the Philippines, with principal office address at 18 Rosal Street, Barangay Capri, Novaliches, Quezon City, herein represented by its duly authorized representative, hereinafter referred to as the “LESSOR”;

— and —

JUAN DELA CRUZ, of legal age, Filipino citizen, with residential address at 123 Sampaguita Street, Quezon City, bearing Government ID No. 1234-5678-9012, hereinafter referred to as the “LESSEE”;

The LESSOR and LESSEE shall be collectively referred to herein as the “Parties.”

W I T N E S S E T H :

WHEREAS, the LESSOR is the owner and/or duly authorized administrator of the residential unit described herein and desires to lease the same to the LESSEE under the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement;

WHEREAS, the LESSEE desires to lease the said residential unit from the LESSOR for residential purposes only;

NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and other good and valuable considerations, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

Article I — Description of Leased Premises

Section 1.01. The LESSOR hereby leases unto the LESSEE the following residential unit, and the LESSEE hereby accepts such lease:

Unit Designation: Sky Terrace — Room No. 301
Location: 4th Floor, 18 Rosal Street, Barangay Capri, Novaliches, Quezon City 1123
Floor Area: Approximately thirty-two (32) square meters
Furnishing: Fully Furnished (air conditioning unit, bed frame, mattress, cabinet, study table, and chair)
Common Areas: Shared kitchen, hallways, lobby, and laundry area

Section 1.02. The LESSEE acknowledges having inspected the leased premises and received the same in good and tenantable condition.

Article II — Term of Lease

Section 2.01. This Agreement shall be effective for a period of three (3) months, commencing on March 1, 2025 and ending on May 31, 2025, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

Section 2.02. At the expiration of the lease term, this Agreement may be renewed upon mutual written agreement of the Parties, subject to such terms and conditions as may then be agreed upon.

Section 2.03. Holdover tenancy beyond the stipulated term without written renewal shall be treated as a month-to-month tenancy subject to the same terms herein, unless either party provides thirty (30) days written notice of termination.

Article III — Rental Rate and Payment

Section 3.01. The LESSEE agrees to pay the LESSOR a monthly rental of THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (₱3,500.00), Philippine Currency, inclusive of electricity, water, and internet connection.

Section 3.02. Rental payments shall be due and payable on or before the 5th day of each calendar month. Payments may be made via GCash, Maya, bank transfer, or cash at the management office.

Section 3.03. A late payment penalty of TWO HUNDRED PESOS (₱200.00) per day shall be imposed for each day of delay beyond the 10th day of the month.

Section 3.04. Upon execution of this Agreement, the LESSEE has paid the LESSOR a Security Deposit equivalent to one (1) month’s rent, amounting to THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (₱3,500.00), receipt of which is hereby acknowledged.

Section 3.05. The security deposit shall not be applied to any monthly rental. It shall be returned within thirty (30) days after lease expiration, less deductions for unpaid rent or damages attributable to the LESSEE.

Article IV — Use of Premises

Section 4.01. The LESSEE shall use the leased premises solely and exclusively for residential purposes. Any commercial, industrial, or other non-residential use is strictly prohibited without prior written consent from the LESSOR.

Section 4.02. The LESSEE shall not conduct any illegal, immoral, or offensive activity on the premises that may disturb the peace, safety, or comfort of other residents or neighboring occupants.

Section 4.03. The LESSEE shall comply with all applicable laws, ordinances, and regulations of the Republic of the Philippines and the local government of Quezon City pertaining to the use of the leased premises.

Article V — Maintenance, Repairs, and Alterations

Section 5.01. The LESSEE shall maintain the leased premises in a clean, sanitary, and habitable condition at all times. The LESSEE shall be responsible for ordinary upkeep, including regular cleaning, proper disposal of garbage, and minor repairs costing less than FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (₱500.00).

Section 5.02. Major repairs necessitated by structural defects, normal wear and tear, or force majeure events shall be for the account of the LESSOR, provided that the LESSEE promptly notifies the LESSOR through the designated maintenance request channel.

Section 5.03. Any damage caused by the negligence, misuse, or willful act of the LESSEE or guests shall be repaired at the LESSEE’s sole expense. If the LESSEE fails to make such repairs, the LESSOR may do so and charge the cost accordingly.

Section 5.04. No structural alterations, improvements, or modifications shall be made to the premises without prior written consent. Any approved improvements shall become property of the LESSOR upon termination without reimbursement.

Article VI — House Rules and Conduct

Section 6.01. The LESSEE acknowledges receipt of and agrees to be bound by the House Rules and Regulations of AMS Property Management, incorporated herein by reference. Key provisions include:

(a) Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM daily. Excessive noise or disruptive behavior is strictly prohibited during these hours;
(b) No Smoking: Strictly prohibited inside the unit, hallways, and all enclosed common areas;
(c) No Pets: Animals of any kind are not permitted within the premises;
(d) Visitors: Overnight guests are permitted for a maximum of three (3) consecutive nights per month and must register at the lobby;
(e) Subletting: The LESSEE shall not assign, sublet, or transfer any rights under this Agreement without prior written approval from the LESSOR.

Section 6.02. Violations of the House Rules may result in written warning, additional charges, or termination of this Agreement at the discretion of the LESSOR.

Article VII — Termination

Section 7.01. Termination by the LESSOR. The LESSOR may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice for: (a) non-payment of rent; (b) material violation of any provision hereof; (c) use of the premises for unlawful purposes; or (d) such other grounds recognized under applicable Philippine law.

Section 7.02. Pre-termination by the LESSEE. Should the LESSEE wish to terminate this Agreement prior to the lease end, at least thirty (30) days advance written notice is required. Pre-termination shall result in forfeiture of the security deposit as liquidated damages.

Section 7.03. Upon termination or expiration, the LESSEE shall surrender the premises in the same condition as received, reasonable wear and tear excepted, and return all keys and access cards to the LESSOR.

Article VIII — Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 8.01. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of the Philippines, particularly Civil Code Articles 1642–1688 on Lease and applicable housing laws.

Section 8.02. Dispute Resolution. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be subject to amicable settlement. If unresolved within fifteen (15) days, the matter shall be submitted to the appropriate courts of Quezon City.

Section 8.03. Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements, whether oral or written.

Section 8.04. Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is declared invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue to be valid and binding upon the Parties.

Section 8.05. Amendments. No amendment or modification of any provision hereof shall be valid unless made in writing and duly signed by both Parties.
